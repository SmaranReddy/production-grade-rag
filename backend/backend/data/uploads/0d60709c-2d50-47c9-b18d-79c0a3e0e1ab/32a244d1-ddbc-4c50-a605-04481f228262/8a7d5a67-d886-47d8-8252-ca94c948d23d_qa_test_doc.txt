Enterprise Knowledge Management Policy

1. Data Classification
All company data must be classified into one of four categories:
- Public: Information approved for external distribution.
- Internal: General business information for employees only.
- Confidential: Sensitive business data requiring restricted access.
- Restricted: Highly sensitive data such as PII and financial records.

2. Access Control
Access to confidential and restricted data requires manager approval.
All access requests must be submitted via the IT helpdesk portal.
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for restricted data access.

3. Retention Policy
Public and internal documents are retained for 3 years.
Confidential documents are retained for 7 years.
Restricted documents are retained for 10 years or as required by law.

4. Incident Response
Any suspected data breach must be reported to the security team within 24 hours.
The incident response team will assess severity and notify affected parties.
Post-incident reviews are mandatory for all Severity 1 incidents.
